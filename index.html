I am a rising junior at the University of Michigan, where I study mathematics & computer science. I am currently a Software Engineering intern at CME Group, and eventually hope to become a Software Engineer, working on things that make a difference in people's lives through beautiful, elegant, well-thought-out algorithms.
I love math! Specifically, I find combinatorics and graph theory very interesting to me. At UM, I am in the Mathematical Sciences Program, and am working on a concentration in Discrete & Algorithmic methods. This past school year, I've enjoyed serving as a math & computer science tutor for the university's Academic Success Program.
I love puzzles. Nothing is more exciting to me than visualizing, discussing, sketching, and banging my head up against a wall over an interesting problem. In my free time, find me making origami dinosaurs, playing with Rubik's cubes, obsessing over 2048, developing software to solve my sudoku for me, etc.
But math, computer science, and puzzles are not my only loves. Of course, I also love Michigan Football. Go Blue!
